Flat Palette

This is a preview of a program I'm making to pick out color palettes for games and modeling.
It's mainly for low poly 3D modeling, as I use that art style a lot in my games.
Eventually I'm planning on letting you import 3D models into it and paint individual faces with colors from the palette.
